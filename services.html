Our Services

We offer free 15-20 minute virtual consultations with owners of old electronics to determine the condition and quality of those electronics, and utilize that information to give them a monetary offer for said electronics. We evaluate based on a set of strict criteria, but electronics we assess do not necessarily need to work, as long as certain criteria are met.

We accept a variety of electronics with some exceptions on microelectronics and devices larger than a desktop computer. Please contact us if you are unsure if your device fits these requirements.
