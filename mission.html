Our Mission

The mission of Sell It Forward is to end the e-waste problem through consumer incentivization.

At Sell It Forward, we see e-waste, or electronic waste, to be a major global issue and one that’s growing fast. Tens of millions of tons of e-waste are generated worldwide every year, polluting the environment with corrosive and harmful chemicals.

Our business model supports a circular economic solution to e-waste, reselling consumers’ outdated electronics to companies that sustainably recycle and reuse components otherwise doomed to the landfill.
